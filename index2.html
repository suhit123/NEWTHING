Home Doctor

Skip the hassle of travel and long waiting queues at hospital by scheduling in-home doctor consultation at your convenience. Get personalised care for you and your family with doctor visits at home for general check-ups, post-operative care, diabetes management, palliative care, pain management and more. Once you pay advance and confirm the visit, your doctor's mobile number and live location updates will be displayed on map till they reach your place.

Tele-Consultation

With Health Sathi App, you can now get genuine feedback and doubts cleared by your preferred specialists. Senior doctors with expertise in their own specialities, will go through your medical history, investigations and existing treatment modalities to give experience based opinions. Once you pay advance to confirm a slot, your doctor will receive details including patient name, age, medical records, assessment details and reach out to you for the online consultation.

Care Taker

Our well-trained and experienced nurses take care of mobility, hygiene, tube feeding, stoma and wound care for patients recovering from complex surgeries. Nursing care also includes managing the entire medication, drug administration, medical dressing and other needs like vaccination. Once you pay advance and confirm the service, details of your provider including name, age, mobile number and live location on map will be displayed with which you can track till they reach your place.

Home Personal Care

With Treat at Home, you and your loved ones can ensure a safe independent living at home. Medicine Delivery

Save the hassle, cost and time of travelling to and fro home and pharmacies. Your medication will be delivered to your doorstep on the same day post-consultation with our doctors.

Lab Test Services

With Health Sathi App, it’s now easy to quickly choose from labs around and book any of the tests available. You can also upload the lab test form given by a doctor and schedule service for your loved ones hassle-free. Only have the option to pay in cash? You’re welcome to choose ‘cash after service’ and reports will be shared in HS app, email after completion. Once you confirm a slot, the lab will receive details including patient name, age, test names or form, address, mobile number and your location for the collection agent to easily navigate.
